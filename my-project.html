My projects

My first project

In my first project I will tell you about my trip to Norway. Me and my sisters went to Norway to earn some money at picking strawberries. We lived in a village named Haukasen, it was 5km from the nearest city. We lived at our employer place in one room. During this month we went for some field trips and also did some sightseeing of Oslo city.

My second project

In my second project I will tell you about my trip to Sweden. Me and my friend Maciej went to Sweden to earn some money at working in a construction company of his fathers. We painted houses of some friends of Maciej's father in Stockholm. Basically we worked in detached houses but one time we had to repair the roof in the twin house. At weekends we were trying to see some new things, so we travelled by car to new places, basically we wanted to do nature exploration so we saw a lot of lakes placed in forests. We worked for 1.5 month and then returned to Poland.

My third project

In my third project I will tell you about my trip to Netherlands. Me and my friend Michał went to Netherlands to earn money at orderpicking for Alberhein Company. We were collecting orders in storehouses for individual Alberhein markets. We lived in the Haag city really close to the sea. We were here for 2 months living at his sister's place. In free time we focused to see as many places as we could. It was easy to achieve because I had my own car which we used for travelling across the Netherlands. We were sightseeing Amsterdam, Rotterdam, we also went to some villages and National Parks. I had a really good time here.
